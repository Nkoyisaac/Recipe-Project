Ingredients

  1. rice
  2. beans
  3. water
  4. oil
  5. salt
  6. onions
  7. tomatoes
  8. tomato paste

cooking method "Beans"

  • boil the beans in a metalic pot until soft enough, (keep adding water until we get to the perfect softness state)
  • Use any type of cooking oil to frie tomato fruit/veg and onions with tomato paste all along and mix it with your boiled Beans
  • add some spicy powder of your choice to give all your mix a flavour of your choice
  • let it boil for a couple more minutes to let the fusion begin and your beans will be ready

cooking method "Rice"

  1. boil your water in a ketlle
  2. put the 100gram of rice in a plastic container
  3. Poor your boilled water in side the plastic container where your rice is located (enough water to cover the surface of the rice)
  4. add spicy powder of your choice
  5. Put in a microwave for 10min and then check it if not soft enough
  6. add more water just to slightly cover the surface let it inside
  7. the microwave for 10 more min your rice will be ready and soft enough to be eaten
